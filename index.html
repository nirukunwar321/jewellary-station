Legacy of the Kathmandu Valley

Sacred Craftsmanship

For centuries, the Newari goldsmiths of Nepal have used the "Lost Wax" method to create deities and adornments. We continue this legacy by blending ancient symbols like the Mandala and Lotus into modern wearable art.

Our gold is sourced ethically, and every 24K piece is hallmark-certified, ensuring that your investment is as pure as the Himalayan snow.
